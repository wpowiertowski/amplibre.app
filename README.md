# amplibre.app
Amplibre brings Winamp back to life on macOS. Pixel-perfect classic skin rendering, 100k+ community skins, 10-band EQ, spectrum visualizer, and full playlist management — all native Swift. Streams from Bandcamp and Apple Music, scrobbles to Last.fm, and backs up your DRM-free library to any drive. Your music, your player, open source.
